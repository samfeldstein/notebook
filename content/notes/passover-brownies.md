## Ingredients

- 1 c butter
- 1 3/4 c sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1/3 c dark cocoa powder
- 3/4 c reg cocoa powder
- 1/2 c potato starch
- 1/4 c almond flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 T fine coffee grounds
- Chocolate chips

### Frosting

- 1/2 c chocolate chips
- 6 T heavy cream
